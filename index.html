Comparisons of the number of votes obtained by candidate and similarity - Candidate Diego Laserna





























    For cases like the candidate of the Alianza Verde party, Diego Laserna (whose election result was 8503 votes) and the candidates closest to him on the map it can be said that according to the results obtained by these, Laserna was able to strengthen his electoral campaign In the popular sectors, in places like Suba and Kennedy, the candidate showed very poor results compared to the other candidates. In Usaquén, on the other hand, his participation was important, showing leadership in the counts (vs the other candidates similar to him). His strategy could have been concentrated in the following fronts to increase few votes, 50 more or less, in each of the localities to have obtained the number of votes obtained by the last elected of the green party, Dora Bastidas (9403 votes).
